ITALY

I want to travel to Italy to visit the famous St. Peter's Basilica. It is one of the holiest temples for Christianity and one of the largest churches in the world. Also, it is where the Pope presides many liturgies all year round. The construction of this church started in 1506 but then the old basilica was torn down. In 1626, it was finished and consecrated on November 18, 1626. The basilica was named after one of Jesus's 12 disciples, Saint Peter, who was then one of the founders of St. Peter's basilica, executed in Rome, and was buried where the catholic church stands now. You can enjoy visiting the church if you roam around the Vatican Grottoes, the Necropolis, and St. Peter's tomb. There are also other tombs of the 91 popes buried there



You can visit St. Peter's Basilica by taking the Rome Metro system or ride a bus. Also, it will only take you 15-20 mins if you want to walk from the heart of Rome to the church. Another place to visit in Italy is the famous Sistine Chapel. It is where the ceremonies of the papal court were held here.
