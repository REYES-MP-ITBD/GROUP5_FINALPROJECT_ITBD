How to improve your SEO


  • Update your website

  • Choose Appropriate Keyword

    •              Choose better keyword for your website to improve the ranking of your website. Consider the type of the page that you have, content, sub-topics popular term that the social media use. You can use some tools from google to gather data about the rank of the word the you may use. For example the word "website" has good rank and it is related to your website, you can use it as your keyword.


  • Relevant Content

    •              This is also a good strategy for your website. You can include some relevant content for your page like the other blogs does. Make your keywords/website broaden about its content, there's so many article,blogs,etc.. that can help your page to improve and have a better keywords to use.


  • Good Backlinks


    •              Other than relevant content, this is also good strategy where you link your website in external website that having different content to create another keyword but still connected to your website. It is ne other major factor search engines consider when deciding how to rank your website is its reputation, measured in the number of quality backlinks to your site.


  • Optimize your website


    •              The speed of your website is also consider to the perfomance of it. Page speed is a direct ranking factor, a fact known even better since Google's Algorithm Speed Update. However, speed can also affect rankings indirectly, by increasing the bounce rate and reducing dwell time.





    Why Search Engine Optimization is important?



           Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is important because it makes your website more visible which can help to improve your website, the more traffic that you get the more opportunities to convert prospects into customers. SEO techniques matter because SEO is made up of multiple different elements, and knowing what they are and how they work is key to understanding why SEO is so important.



  • Authority

    •            The concept of authority is relatively new in SEO, but it’s becoming increasingly important to search engines because it’s becoming more important to web users. Essentially, authority means that your website is trustworthy, high quality, relevant, and has something to offer. The more authority your site has, the higher your ranking will be, and the more prospects will come to trust your brand
