About Landing Page


Landing pages can be a great benefit to any business trying to attract clients on-line. A number of on-line services offer templates for creating landing pages with proven effectiveness, while custom-made landing pages offer a personal touch. Regardless, it is important to keep a few things in mind. The headline should draw the viewers attention and the introductory text should be relatively short to avoid overwhelming the potential customer before effectively present the companys service or product. Lists are a great way to summarize the benefits of the product or service offered. Images and the use of distracting backgrounds or hard-to-read fonts should be minimized. Finally, including a simple yet inviting form that prompts clients to include their contact information in order to receive the weekly email or further information is a great way to create email lists using a landing page. An effective landing page is an important marketing tool that not only boosts interest but also provides feedback regarding a particular companys offerings.
