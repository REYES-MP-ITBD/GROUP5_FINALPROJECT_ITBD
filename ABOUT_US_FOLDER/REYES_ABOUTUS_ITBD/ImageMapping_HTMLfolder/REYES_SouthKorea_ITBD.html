SOUTH KOREA

Nami Island is one of the most popular attractions in South Korea. Featuring beautiful tree lanes, woodlands, riverside walks, and many more. The incredible scenery here has been used as shooting locations of some Korean dramas. The good thing about Nami Island is that it offers something special for everyone in every season. You can enjoy the beautiful scenery of springtime as the flowers here begin to bloom. While summertime brings lush greenery scenery to the island. Have fun strolling in the tree lanes. From mid-September to November, you can witness the falling of leaves in Nami Island. While the magical feel in Nami Island will be in the wintertime. The snow-covered tree lanes made the scenery one of the shooting locations in Korea. It gives a romantic winter environment.



Going to Nami Island can be via ITX or high-speed train, subway in Yongsan Station, or a private car. I also want to visit Jinhae Cherry Blossom Festival. I want to experience and witness there fun-filled festival of Cherry Blossom.
