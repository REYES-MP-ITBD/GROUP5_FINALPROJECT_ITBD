social media marketing platform: instagram

As Covid-19 is still out there, it massively affects our daily lives. For your business, you can take the risk of managing your business with face-to-face interaction, or you can settle for now with the idea of digital marketing. There are a lot of social media platforms you can use for advertising your business digitally, one of those is Instagram.

Instagram was developed in 2009 by a student from Stanford University named Kevin Systrom. It was launched on 6 October 2010 that reached 25,000 users on its first day. Instagram's original feature was for posting photographs taken by mobile devices. Additional features came that made Instagram more fascinating and beneficial to marketers. Instagram announced last 1 June 2016 the availability of the business profiles and tools as its new features. That made Instagram one of the social media platforms used for digital marketing.

This social media platform has a lot of business tools you can use to promote your business to the online world. The first thing you need to is you must switch your profile into a business account on Instagram before using the business tools offered. You will need a Facebook page to sign up for a business account on Instagram. Make sure that you already have one so you can start exploring the business tools on Instagram. (pics about steps)

After switching your profile to a business account, you can now use all the business features available on Instagram. Here are the business profile features offered:

  1. Instagram Insights. It is considered the data analytics tool on Instagram. You'll be able to understand who your audience is, what are the type of content they engage the most, and when are they most active. With this, you'll be able to create content and make your advertising strategies effective to your audience. These are some of the features under Instagram Insights:
    • Audience Analytics - knowing the age range, gender, and location of the people who follows and likes your posts.
    • Follower Growth Metrics - monitor whether your follower increases or decreases.
    • Most Active Times Metrics - you have an idea on what time your audience are most active.
    • Detailed Story Performance - you'll be able to know how many views does your Instragam stories have. You can also see here the number of times your audience exits your story, sent replies, and other.
  2. Call-to-Action buttons. Call-to-Action feature help to facilitate the interaction with followers. It makes your services easily navigated by your audience.
  3. Promoted Posts. One of the powerful advertising tools in Instagram. You can promote the posts you have created and pay to distribute it to your chosen target audience.
  4. Instagram Shoppable Posts. You can sync your store in Instagram and create shoppable posts. You can now share posts with direct links to your website or business page. This feature makes it easy for your audience to explore your profile and engaged them more with your products / services.
  5. IG Live and Shopping. Instagram Live brings people together and interact with one another. It strengthens your relationship with the community you have established on Instagram. IG Live can also serve as your marketing platform and promote your business live.
