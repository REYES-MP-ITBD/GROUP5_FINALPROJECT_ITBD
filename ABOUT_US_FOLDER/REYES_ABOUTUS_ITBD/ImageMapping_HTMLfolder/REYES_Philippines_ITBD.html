PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is made up of 7,641 islands, Amanpulo is one of the treasured islands and has repeatedly been called the most beautiful one in our country. The island of Amanpulo is known to be the "peaceful paradise" because of its calming environment and breath-taking view. A place where you can achieve inner peace, savor the beauty of nature, and enjoy fun-filled adventures. It is a private island in Pamalican, Palawan fringed with white-sand and pristine reefs. It offers aquatic adventures from kite surfing and scuba diving, to snorkeling and kayaking. Other activities like tennis, cycling, bird watching, and jungle obstacle course are also offered in Amanpulo Island. At night, you can get close to the stars with the astronomical refracting telescope. Since Amanpulo is located in Palawan, you can either go there via international flights in Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) or private charters accommodated by Amanpulo Reservations.
