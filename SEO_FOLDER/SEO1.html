Search Engine Optimization



What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization.It is how you attract people by improving your website and increase its visitor where practicing the increase of quantity and quality of traffic to your website. This is the process of optimizing your website to get organic, or un-paid, traffic from the search engine results page.


  • Organic Search Engine Optimization

    •              Organic search traffic is specifically any unpaid traffic that comes from SERPs. You don't have to pay for it instead you have to work hard for it by using method and strategies to gain visitor in order to get high search rank in search engin result pages (SERPs).


  • Quantity of Traffic

    •              It is the result that the user click on your website from those search engine result pages(SERPs).


  • Quality of Traffic

    •              It is more about attracting the users visit your website. It is not because the search engine result is recommended your site but it is because of the product or the website offer they are very interested to look at it and visit your page. If the user visit your website because of the recommendation it is not counted as quality traffic.



    How's SEO Works?

    Web search tools, for example, Google and Bing use bots to gather data pages on the web, going from one website to another, gathering data about those pages and placing them in a file. Search engines has a crawler that goes out and gathers information about all the content they can find on the Internet. The crawlers bring all those 1s and 0s back to the search engine to build an index. That index is then fed through an algorithm that tries to match all that data with your query.In order to do this, search engines will scan, or crawl, different websites to better understand what the site is about. For example, content quality and keyword research are key factors of content optimization, and crawlability and mobile-friendliness are important site architecture factors.Optimizing your website has a factor and can help to increase your website and gain higher rank in search result.
