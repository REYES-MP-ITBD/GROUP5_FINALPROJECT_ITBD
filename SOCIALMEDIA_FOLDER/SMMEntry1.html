social media in digital marketing

Maria Patrice Reyes â€¢ 14 April 2021

Almost everyone uses social media applications as means of communication, entertainment, and even in marketing now. Since our society was affected positively or negatively by social media, it's still an effective way to advertise your business online.

Social Media Marketing is advertising your business with the use of social media platforms. It is a digital marketing technique that uses social media platforms to attract and gain targeted customers. This advertising technique is cost-effective, quite time-consuming, and directly goes to your targeted audience or specific community you have online. It has different roles that will help your business grow:

  • Direct Interaction with customers. We spend most of our time on social media platforms; it is where the people are. You'll be able to attract a massive number of potential customers with the use of social media. Another good thing here is that people can get all the information about your business all the time.
  • Branding Platform. Social media can help you increase brand recognition and improve the loyalty of your brand. You have to make a reputation to inform your audience about the online presence of your business. It will help your business to be easily accessible for your potential customers. Also, branding on social media aims to boost awareness regarding your business. Amidst all the noises in social media, it can be hard to get noticed. Be creative in creating your content and advertisements to get more attention.
  • Modern Customer Service Channel. With social media, you can provide real-time two-way communication with your customers regarding their concerns or complaints. Customers are more likely engaged to business that responds quickly in complaints. They also tend to transfer their loyalty to other brands when they get disappointed over a social media conversation with a brand.

Social Media platforms can be a great help to the growth of your business. Choosing the right platforms and create eye-catching advertisements will attract more potential customers. You can easily disseminate information about your business to make a reputation in the online world.
